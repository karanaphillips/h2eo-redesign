Frequently Asked Questions

What is Module 1 for free?

Every course starts with a free introductory module. You can explore the content, complete the activities, and even do the 7-day challenge — all without paying anything. It's our way of letting you experience the platform before committing.

What's the difference between K-12 and Adult pricing?

K-12 students get a subscription model — one monthly or annual fee unlocks all courses. Adult learners purchase individual courses once and keep access forever. This reflects how each group typically engages with learning content.

Can I switch from K-12 to Adult (or vice versa)?

Yes — your account role can be updated in your profile settings. Contact support if you need help migrating an existing subscription or purchase.

Is this a real payment or demo mode?

If the Stripe payment links haven't been configured yet by the site owner, you'll see "Demo Mode" when checking out. This lets you simulate a purchase for testing. Real payments require the Stripe links to be set in the configuration file.

What happens after I pay?

You'll be redirected back to Hero LMS automatically and your access will be activated immediately. No waiting, no manual steps — just start learning.

Do I need a Google or Microsoft account?

No — you can also sign in with your email address. Google and Microsoft OAuth are available as convenient options, but they're not required.
