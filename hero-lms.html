How It Works

Three Steps to Personalized Learning

Most platforms dump you into a catalog. Hero LMS builds your path before you take a single lesson.

1

Discover Your Superpowers™

Take the Academic Superpowers™ assessment — 28 forced-choice questions, about 5 minutes. You get a detailed cognitive profile: your archetype, how you process information, and what learning environments bring out your best work.

2

Get a Profile-Matched Learning Path

Hero LMS uses your profile to surface courses, sequences, and study strategies that fit how you think — not a generic bestseller list. Every recommendation is tagged to an archetype and explained in terms of why it suits you.

3

Learn With an AI Coach in Your Corner

As you progress, your AI coach checks in, adapts suggestions, and helps you apply what you're learning to real situations. It knows your archetype — so the advice actually fits you, not a hypothetical average learner.
