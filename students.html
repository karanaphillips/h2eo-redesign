Your Unique Strengths

What's Your Academic Superpower™?

Every learner has a dominant way of thinking, engaging, and making sense of the world. The Academic Superpowers™ framework identifies yours — so everything from how you study to the courses you take can be aligned to your strengths.

Hands-on Hero

You learn by doing

You thrive in labs, workshops, and projects. You grasp concepts quickly when you can try them out — and you lose interest fast when forced to sit still and listen.

Mastermind

You learn by strategizing

You see the big picture before the details. You love debate, case studies, and systems. You need context and autonomy — not just step-by-step instructions.

Creative Crusader

You learn by connecting

You think in stories, relationships, and purpose. Collaborative, creative, and mission-driven — you need to understand WHY before you'll commit to HOW.

The Brainiac

You learn by analyzing

You go deep, not wide. Research, complexity, and mastery are your natural habitat. You need time to think — and access to the full picture before you commit.
