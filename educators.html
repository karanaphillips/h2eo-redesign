Quick Reference

The Four Archetypes in Your Classroom

Hands-on Hero

Best teaching approach

Labs, projects, simulations. Clear steps with immediate feedback. Reduce lecture time — increase doing time.

Mastermind

Best teaching approach

Case studies, debate, strategic problems. Give them autonomy and access to the big picture before the details.

Creative Crusader

Best teaching approach

Collaboration, open-ended challenges, multimedia. Connect learning to purpose and real-world impact.

Brainiac

Best teaching approach

Deep-dive resources, research projects, analytical tasks. Give them time to think — and comprehensive reference materials.
