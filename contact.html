Frequently Asked Questions

What services are currently available?

We offer K-12 tutoring, adult tutoring, academic and learning coaching, study skills workshops, and the Academic Superpowers™ assessment with personalized debriefs.

How do I get started?

Complete the contact form with your area of interest and goals. Our team will follow up by email with next steps and scheduling options within 1–2 business days.

Can adults take the Academic Superpowers™ assessment?

Absolutely. Adults can take the assessment and use results to guide tutoring, coaching, and professional growth planning. We have adult-specific archetypes too.

Do you offer workshops for groups or organizations?

Yes. We offer study skills and learning strategy workshops for schools, learning pods, community groups, and corporate teams of all sizes.

What is the Adult Professional Growth Series?

A focused adult learning series designed to build practical growth strategies — including AI readiness and cognitive leadership — through coaching-centered support.

Do you offer enterprise or institutional pricing?

Yes. We offer custom solutions for organizations, schools, and institutions. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and we'll create a tailored proposal.
